REMIX



I honestly had no idea what to do with this. The beginning of the semester was a rush of ideas form a new major and a new environment, that I didn’t know how to make sense of the assignments given to me. It was difficult to understand the entire idea behind the assignments and it showed. When going over other people’s work, I see so much meaning behind their art. There are other feelings in there than just what’s on the surface, and I felt that in terms of narrative, my remix falls short.

Overall, despite my comparisons, I think my remix was good. People caught on to what I meant with the image pretty quickly, and I am glad that it was easily understandable. So much of the world is passing by while we are on our phones, but we need to get off the screens that capture so much of our attention and replace it with face to face socializing. Only then when we look up will we realize the life around us, both the good and the bad.
