SHORT FILMS

THE RICHEST MAN IN THE WORLD (2018)

Near the beginning of the spring 2018 semester in San Jose State, I began writing a short film that I had no idea how to write. It involved themes that I had little to no experience in, and the only experience in film making I had was a short film in 2015 that I felt didn't succeed in its purpose. So making this new, extremely ambitious one was a bit of a stretch. I figured that because I was older and perhaps wiser, I'd be able to make a good film without that said experience.

What ended up happening is that my Digital Media class required a short video instead. So in order to manage my time, I stopped writing that film and instead focused on a short script for what will be this film.

Making this film was not only a blast to make, but also a huge blessing and fountain of experience. I learned so much about how to write a film, as well as direct one too. It helped me understand that not all ideas are communicated in an the manner the writer always wants them to. It also helped me understand that checkking the camera between every shot is (REALLY) important.
